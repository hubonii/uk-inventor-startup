# Prioritized Research Roadmap

This roadmap orders our critical unknowns based on their potential to kill the startup (highest risk to lowest).

## Priority 1: Legal & Employment Viability
**Risk Reduction:** 40% (Existential)
- **Task:** Finalize the exact legal defense for the "commuter-share" classification.
- **Action:** Read the complete UK Supreme Court ruling on Uber drivers. Draft a comparison matrix showing why our intent-based UI differs fundamentally from Uber's algorithmic dispatch.

## Priority 2: Marketplace Liquidity (The Wedge)
**Risk Reduction:** 30% (Existential)
- **Task:** Validate the University Campus rollout strategy.
- **Action:** Calculate the specific daily commuter volume between UCL dorms and the main campus. Map the local delivery demand (e.g., Vinted sellers on campus).

## Priority 3: Unit Economics & API Costs
**Risk Reduction:** 15% (High)
- **Task:** Validate if the £3-£5 model is mathematically possible.
- **Action:** Contact Onfido/Checkr for API pricing on high-volume Liveness checks. Build a financial model simulating 1,000 daily deliveries.

## Priority 4: GDPR & Biometric Data
**Risk Reduction:** 10% (High)
- **Task:** Design the data retention architecture.
- **Action:** Draft a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) template for storing biometric Liveness scans and package content photos.

## Priority 5: Technical Edge Cases
**Risk Reduction:** 5% (Medium)
- **Task:** Solve the "Offline QR Code" problem.
- **Action:** Research cryptographic offline token exchange methods for the double QR-code scan when deep inside the London Underground without signal.
