# Comprehensive Research Roadmap

This document outlines all external research required to de-risk the startup. The research tasks are categorized by domain and prioritized by their impact on business viability.

## 🏆 Executive Priority & Risk Reduction

If we only have time to research three things, they must be these. Solving them reduces the startup's existential risk by 80%:
1. **Legal (Gig Economy Defense):** Reduces risk by 40%. If we fail here, the business model is illegal.
2. **Financial (Unit Economics):** Reduces risk by 25%. If API and Stripe fees consume the £1 margin, the business is unprofitable.
3. **UK Innovator Founder Visa:** Reduces risk by 15%. If the UK Home Office rejects the business plan, the founder cannot operate.

---

## ⚖️ Legal (Existential Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 40% | Priority: Critical*
- **The Uber Test:** Research the exact legal phrasing in the UK Supreme Court ruling (*Uber BV v Aslam*) regarding "worker control." We must confirm if "intent-based matching without penalties" is a recognized legal defense for Information Society Services.
- **Contraband Liability:** Research the "Safe Harbor" provisions under UK law for digital platforms whose users transport illegal goods.

## 💷 Financial (High Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 25% | Priority: Critical*
- **Stripe Connect Fees:** Determine the exact transaction fee structure for micro-payments (£5.00). Research if Stripe offers volume discounts or alternative payment routing to preserve our £1.00 margin.
- **Onfido API Pricing:** Get enterprise quotes for Biometric Face Match and Identity Verification APIs.
- **Micro-Insurance:** Find an insurance broker willing to underwrite P2P transit for 50p per package.

## 🇬🇧 UK Innovator Founder Visa (High Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 15% | Priority: High*
- **Endorsing Bodies:** Research the list of approved UK Endorsing Bodies. Identify which ones focus on Green Tech, Logistics, or B2B SaaS.
- **Innovation Criteria:** Research how Endorsing Bodies evaluate "Innovation" and "Scalability" specifically for marketplaces, to ensure our pitch highlights the PostGIS engine and ESG tailwinds.

## 🛍️ Market (Medium Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 5% | Priority: Medium*
- **ESG Grants:** Research Transport for London (TfL) or Innovate UK grants available for carbon-neutral, zero-emission logistics startups.
- **Market Sizing:** Find concrete data on the volume of daily C2C parcel shipments (Vinted/Depop) within London Zone 1 and 2.

## 👥 Customers (Medium Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 5% | Priority: Medium*
- **Price Elasticity:** Research what the average Vinted seller currently pays for Evri vs Royal Mail, and what premium they assign to "Same-Day" delivery.
- **Courier Demographics:** Research the daily commute volume of UCL students to determine if the "University Wedge" strategy provides enough mathematical density.

## ⚔️ Competitors (Medium Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 5% | Priority: Medium*
- **Vinted Shipping Integrations:** Research if Vinted allows sellers to manually fulfill orders with a custom tracking link, or if they force sellers to use Evri/InPost labels.
- **Failed Attempts:** Analyze startups like Nimber or TaskRabbit to pinpoint exactly why peer-to-peer delivery hasn't dominated urban logistics yet.

## 🔐 Security (Low Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 2% | Priority: Low*
- **QR Replay Attacks:** Research the best libraries for implementing Time-based One-Time Passwords (TOTP) inside dynamic QR codes on React Native.
- **Deepfake Liveness:** Research the failure rate of Onfido's Liveness checks against modern AI-generated deepfakes.

## ⚙️ Technology (Low Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 2% | Priority: Low*
- **Offline BLE Exchange:** Research the reliability of Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) token exchanges on budget Android devices for offline Underground handshakes.
- **App Store Policies:** Research Apple's App Store Review Guidelines regarding apps that require persistent background location tracking for non-employees.

## 🛡️ GDPR (Low Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 1% | Priority: Low*
- **ICO Guidelines:** Research the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) specific guidelines on storing biometric data for anti-fraud purposes.

## 📦 Operations (Low Impact)
*Risk Reduction: 1% | Priority: Low*
- **Tamper-Evident Bags:** Research wholesale suppliers in the UK or China for customized, serialized transparent tamper-evident bags, and calculate the cost-per-unit at 100,000 volume.
