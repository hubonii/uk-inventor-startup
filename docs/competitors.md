# Competitor Analysis

## 🚚 Traditional Logistics (DHL, Hermes/Evri, Royal Mail)
- **Strengths:** Massive infrastructure, high trust, established routing.
- **Weaknesses:** Highly polluting, slow (next day or 48hr standard), expensive for same-day, rigid systems.
- **Our Edge:** We offer true same-day delivery at a lower cost with a zero-carbon footprint by utilizing existing movement.

## 🛵 On-Demand Delivery (Uber Connect, Deliveroo, Stuart)
- **Strengths:** High liquidity, fast delivery, strong brand awareness.
- **Weaknesses:** Forces couriers into stressful, gamified loops. Highly expensive for the sender. Plagued by UK gig-economy employment lawsuits.
- **Our Edge:** "Intent-based" matching removes courier burnout. Our model acts as a "commuter-share" (like BlaBlaCar) rather than a formal transport employer, theoretically protecting us from Uber-style gig economy rulings.

## 👥 P2P Delivery Attempts (Nimber, etc.)
- **Strengths:** Similar core concept.
- **Weaknesses:** Usually fail due to lack of trust, theft, and the "chicken-and-egg" liquidity problem.
- **Our Edge:** Unprecedented security protocols (Biometrics, DBS, Tamper-Evident packaging, visual inspection).
