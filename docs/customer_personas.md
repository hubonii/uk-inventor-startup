# Customer Personas

## 👤 The Commuter (Courier)
**Profile:** A daily metro or bus rider, or a walking commuter. 
**Motivation:** Wants to offset the cost of their TfL travelcard or earn pocket money without changing their routine.
**Pain Points:** Dislikes the high-stress, algorithm-chasing nature of Uber/Deliveroo. Doesn't want to go out of their way.
**Platform Need:** Seamless UX to input their destination and quickly grab a package that adds < 5 minutes to their journey.

## 👤 The C2C Seller (Sender)
**Profile:** A frequent seller on Vinted, Depop, or Facebook Marketplace.
**Motivation:** Needs to get an item to a local buyer quickly but doesn't want to pay £10 for Uber Connect or wait 3 days for Evri.
**Pain Points:** Packaging hassle, high shipping costs eating into margins.
**Platform Need:** Cheap, reliable, same-day delivery with tracking to prove the item was sent.

## 👤 The Local Retailer (Sender)
**Profile:** A boutique shop or local pharmacy.
**Motivation:** Wants to offer same-day delivery to local customers to compete with Amazon.
**Pain Points:** Cannot afford a dedicated delivery driver.
**Platform Need:** Batching capabilities (sending 5 packages out with different commuters) and secure chain-of-custody.
