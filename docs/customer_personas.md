# Customer Personas

## Demand Side (Senders)

### 1. The Vinted Power-Seller (Chloe)
- **Profile:** 22-year-old student, sells 10+ items of clothing a week.
- **Pain Point:** Hates walking to the Evri drop-off point in the rain. Hates that buyers complain about slow Royal Mail delivery.
- **Value Prop:** Someone picks up the package directly from her dorm room and delivers it the same day for the exact same price the buyer paid for standard shipping.

### 2. The Local Artisan Shop (David)
- **Profile:** 40-year-old owner of a specialty coffee roaster in Hackney.
- **Pain Point:** Wants to offer same-day delivery to his London subscribers, but traditional couriers charge £12, destroying his margin on a £15 bag of coffee.
- **Value Prop:** Batch uploads 20 daily deliveries at £4 each.

## Supply Side (Couriers)

### 3. The Cash-Strapped Commuter (Liam)
- **Profile:** 20-year-old student commuting from Zone 3 to Zone 1 every day for lectures.
- **Pain Point:** The Tube pass is expensive (£150/month). Needs beer money but doesn't have time for a scheduled shift at McDonald's.
- **Value Prop:** Opens the app while waiting for the train. Grabs a package heading to his destination. Earns £5. No boss, no uniform, no commitment.
