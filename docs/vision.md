# Vision

## 🌍 The Big Picture
To revolutionize urban logistics by transforming every daily commuter into a potential delivery node, achieving carbon-neutral, same-day delivery through the optimization of existing human movement.

## 🎯 Our Mission
To build the most secure, eco-friendly, and intent-based P2P delivery network in the UK, empowering everyday commuters to monetize their daily routes while drastically reducing urban delivery emissions.

## 🔭 Future State
In 5 years, we envision a city where dedicated delivery vans are obsolete for small-to-medium packages. Instead, packages flow organically through the city via the metro, buses, and pedestrians, guided by our intelligent matching algorithm.
