## 2. Investment Memo

### Thesis
The urban last-mile delivery market has been decimated by cash-burning fleet models (Gorillas, Buyk, Fridge No More). The surviving players (Deliveroo, UberEats) focus exclusively on restaurant delivery. This leaves 580,000+ UK independent retailers without an affordable same-day delivery solution.

We are not building another courier company. We are building the **trust infrastructure** that allows any merchant to safely use any local gig-worker for delivery. Our Collateralized Staking + TOTP Handshake protocol eliminates the need for employee background checks, fleet management, or logistics insurance.

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM:** £12B UK last-mile delivery market
- **SAM:** £2.1B independent retail same-day delivery (pharmacies, hardware, print shops)
- **SOM:** £42M — 20 university wedges × 2,000 merchants × £49/mo SaaS + platform fees

### Competitive Advantage
We are the only platform that provides a **zero-trust cryptographic handshake** specifically designed for unvetted pedestrian/cyclist gig-workers. Incumbent delivery platforms cannot adopt our Collateralized Staking model because it contradicts their employer/contractor fleet model.

## 5. SAFE Note Strategy

### Recommended Terms
- **Instrument:** SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) — UK-adapted via SeedLegals
- **Valuation Cap:** £3,000,000
- **Discount:** 20% on next priced round
- **Pro-Rata Rights:** Yes (for investors contributing >£50k)
- **MFN Clause:** Yes (Most Favored Nation — if we issue better terms later, early investors automatically get them)

### Why SAFE over Convertible Note (UK Context)
| Factor | SAFE | Convertible Note |
|---|---|---|
| Interest | None | Accrues (complicates conversion) |
| Maturity Date | None | Has one (creates pressure/default risk) |
| UK Legal Complexity | Simple (SeedLegals template) | More complex (loan agreement) |
| Investor Familiarity | Growing rapidly in UK | Traditional but declining |
| **Recommendation** | **✅ Use SAFE** | Fallback only |
