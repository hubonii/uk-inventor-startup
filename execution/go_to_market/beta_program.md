# Beta Program — Local-Logistics Protocol

## Program Overview

The Local-Logistics Beta Program is a **6-week structured testing phase** designed to stress-test the cryptographic chain-of-custody protocol with real users before public launch. Beta participants are co-creators — not just testers — and their feedback directly shapes product decisions.

**Program Dates:** Weeks 1–6 of operations (aligned with Soft Launch)
**Cohort Size:** 50 senders, 20 couriers, 10 merchants
**Geography:** 0.5-mile radius around UCL Main Quad, Bloomsbury

## 3. Feedback Loops

### 3.1 Continuous In-App Feedback

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
│  After Every Drop (All Users)        │
│                                      │
│  ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐  Rate this experience     │
│                                      │
│  Quick tags:                         │
│  [Too slow] [Smooth] [App bug]       │
│  [Courier great] [Hard to find]      │
│                                      │
│  Optional: Tell us more... ________  │
│                                      │
│  [Submit]                            │
└──────────────────────────────────────┘
```

- **Trigger:** Appears 2 minutes after delivery confirmation
- **Completion rate target:** > 60%
- **Data stored:** Rating, tags, free text, timestamp, drop ID
- **Alert:** Any 1-star rating triggers Slack notification to founder

### 3.2 Weekly Surveys (All User Types)

| Week | Sender Focus | Courier Focus | Merchant Focus |
|------|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | Onboarding experience, app clarity | First-drop experience, earning clarity | Setup ease, dashboard usability |
| 2 | Pricing perception, speed satisfaction | Route quality, earnings vs expectations | Order volume, packaging workflow |
| 3 | Trust in protocol (TOTP), repeat intent | Staking experience, shift flexibility | Customer feedback received |
| 4 | Feature requests, comparison to alternatives | Earnings optimisation, app reliability | Integration with existing workflow |
| 5 | Referral willingness, NPS | Community/belonging, career potential | Expansion interest, pricing feedback |
| 6 | Overall satisfaction, retention intent | Overall satisfaction, continuation intent | ROI assessment, expansion intent |

**Survey specs:**
- Delivered via email + in-app notification every Monday 9 AM
- Maximum 8 questions per survey (< 2 minutes)
- Mix of Likert scale, multiple choice, and one open-ended question
- Completion incentivised with credit (see incentives above)
- Results compiled by Tuesday EOD into weekly dashboard

### 3.3 Net Promoter Score (NPS) Tracking

| Measurement Point | Method | Target NPS |
|-------------------|--------|-----------|
| After first drop | In-app popup | ≥ 30 (new user excitement) |
| Week 3 midpoint | Email survey | ≥ 35 (settling in) |
| Week 6 exit | Structured interview | ≥ 50 (validated value) |

**NPS Categories:**
- **Promoters (9–10):** Invite to referral program, request app store review, feature in case study
- **Passives (7–8):** Send personalised "what would make us a 10?" email from founder
- **Detractors (0–6):** Founder calls within 24 hours; log issue in product backlog

### 3.4 1:1 Deep-Dive Calls

| User Type | Frequency | Duration | Interviewer | Format |
|-----------|-----------|----------|-------------|--------|
| Senders (top 10 by volume) | Bi-weekly | 15 min | Founder | Video call |
| Couriers (all 20) | Weekly (Weeks 1–2), bi-weekly after | 20 min | Ops lead | In-person or phone |
| Merchants (all 10) | Weekly | 15 min | Founder | In-person visit |

**1:1 Call Template:**
1. "What's working really well?" (2 min)
2. "What's the most frustrating thing?" (3 min)
3. "Walk me through your last drop — what happened?" (5 min)
4. "If you could change one thing, what would it be?" (2 min)
5. "Would you recommend this to a friend? Why/why not?" (2 min)
6. "Anything else on your mind?" (1 min)

### 3.5 Observational Research

| Method | Frequency | Participants |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Courier ride-alongs | 3 per week (Weeks 1–3) | Founder shadows courier for full shift |
| Merchant observation | 2 per week (Weeks 1–4) | Founder sits in pharmacy during dispatch window |
| Screen recording (with consent) | Ongoing | 10 senders opt-in to Hotjar-style session recording |
| WhatsApp beta group | Daily | All beta users in a group chat for real-time feedback |

## 5. Beta Timeline

```
Week 1  ──── Onboarding Week
              • All users onboarded & complete first drop
              • Daily check-ins with every courier
              • Founder shadows 5 deliveries

Week 2  ──── Stabilisation
              • Fix critical bugs from Week 1
              • First weekly survey deployed
              • Courier staking deposit soft-introduced

Week 3  ──── Growth Sprint
              • Activate referral codes for beta senders
              • Second merchant cohort onboarded (5 more)
              • Mid-beta NPS measurement

Week 4  ──── Feature Testing
              • Test surge pricing during peak hours (12–2 PM)
              • Test merchant batch-dispatch feature
              • Courier tier system soft-launch (Bronze/Silver)

Week 5  ──── Stress Testing
              • Push for 25+ drops/day
              • Simulate edge cases (courier cancellation, TOTP expiry)
              • Run first "merchant analytics" report

Week 6  ──── Exit & Evaluation
              • Exit interviews with all participants
              • Final NPS measurement
              • Beta graduation assessment
              • Plan Hard Launch based on learnings
```

## 6. Graduation Criteria

### 6.1 Product Graduation (Must Pass All)

| Criterion | Threshold | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| App crash rate | < 1% of sessions | ☐ |
| TOTP handshake success rate | ≥ 98% | ☐ |
| Average delivery time | ≤ 25 minutes | ☐ |
| Delivery completion rate | ≥ 95% | ☐ |
| Critical bugs outstanding | 0 P0 bugs, < 3 P1 bugs | ☐ |
| Payment processing success | ≥ 99% | ☐ |

### 6.2 Market Graduation (Must Pass ≥ 4 of 6)

| Criterion | Threshold | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Sender NPS | ≥ 40 | ☐ |
| Courier NPS | ≥ 30 | ☐ |
| Merchant NPS | ≥ 50 | ☐ |
| Sender repeat rate (≥ 2 drops) | ≥ 40% of beta senders | ☐ |
| Courier retention (still active Week 6) | ≥ 70% of starting couriers | ☐ |
| Merchant continuation intent | ≥ 80% say "yes, I'd continue" | ☐ |

### 6.3 Economics Graduation (Must Pass All)

| Criterion | Threshold | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Gross margin per drop | ≥ £0.30 (positive) | ☐ |
| Courier earnings per hour | ≥ £10 (above minimum wage) | ☐ |
| Sender willingness to pay (stated) | ≥ 70% accept current pricing | ☐ |
| Staking pool claims | < 2% of total drops | ☐ |
| Platform fee collection rate | ≥ 95% | ☐ |

### 6.4 Graduation Decision Matrix

| All Product ✓ | Market ≥ 4/6 | All Economics ✓ | Decision |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|-----------|
| ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | **GRADUATE** → Hard Launch |
| ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | Extend beta 2 weeks; adjust pricing |
| ✅ | ❌ | ✅ | Extend beta 2 weeks; improve UX/experience |
| ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | Extend beta 4 weeks; significant product iteration |
| ❌ | Any | Any | **DO NOT LAUNCH** — fix product stability first |

## 7. Post-Beta Transition

### For Graduating Beta Users
- **Senders:** Automatic transition to live platform; "Founding Sender" badge permanent
- **Couriers:** Automatic transition; maintain earned tier status; staking deposit required if not already
- **Merchants:** Transition to paid plan (£49/mo) with 1 month free as thank-you

### Beta Learnings Documentation
- **Product backlog:** All feedback items prioritised and scheduled for next 3 sprints
- **Case studies:** 3 user stories (1 sender, 1 courier, 1 merchant) written up for marketing
- **Metrics report:** Full beta metrics compiled into investor-ready document
- **Process improvements:** All operational learnings documented in internal wiki
