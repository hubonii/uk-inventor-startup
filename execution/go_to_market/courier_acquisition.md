## 2. Onboarding Funnel

### 2.1 Funnel Overview

```
STAGE 1: DOWNLOAD                    Conversion
  App download from QR/link          ─── 100%
        │
        ▼
STAGE 2: SIGNUP                       ─── 85%
  Name, email, phone, postcode
        │
        ▼
STAGE 3: KYC VERIFICATION            ─── 70%
  ID upload + selfie match
  Right-to-work check (Onfido)
  Background check (basic DBS)
        │
        ▼
STAGE 4: ONBOARDING TRAINING         ─── 90%
  15-min in-app tutorial
  TOTP protocol walkthrough
  Safety & conduct guidelines
  Quiz: 5 questions, must score 4/5
        │
        ▼
STAGE 5: FIRST DROP                   ─── 80%
  Accept first available drop
  Optional: shadowed by experienced courier
  £5 bonus on completion
        │
        ▼
STAGE 6: STAKING UNLOCK              ─── 75%
  After 5 completed drops
  Deposit £50 stake
  Unlock: priority matching, premium drops, tier system
        │
        ▼
ACTIVE COURIER                        ─── 60% of downloaders
```

**Overall funnel conversion: Download → Active Courier = ~32%**

### 2.2 Stage Details

#### Stage 1–2: Download & Signup (Target: < 3 minutes)
- App available on iOS App Store and Google Play
- Deep link from all recruitment channels (QR, URL, social)
- Signup fields: First name, last name, email, phone, postcode, profile photo
- No payment info required at signup
- Immediate access to "courier preview mode" — can see live drops in area (but can't accept)

#### Stage 3: KYC Verification (Target: < 24 hours)
- **ID Verification:** Passport or UK driving licence scan via Onfido
- **Selfie Match:** Liveness check + photo match to ID
- **Right-to-Work:** Automated check against Home Office database
- **Basic DBS:** Standard criminal record check (£23 cost, covered by platform for first 100 couriers)
- **Address Verification:** Must be within 3 miles of operating zone
- **Failure handling:** Clear rejection reason + appeal process (manual review within 48 hrs)

#### Stage 4: Onboarding Training (Target: 15 minutes)
**In-app interactive tutorial covering:**

| Module | Duration | Content |
|--------|----------|---------|
| 1. How It Works | 3 min | Animated walkthrough of a drop: accept → collect → deliver → confirm |
| 2. TOTP Protocol | 4 min | How QR scanning works: generating codes, scanning at pickup, scanning at delivery |
| 3. Earnings & Pricing | 3 min | How True Marketplace pricing works: you set your rate, platform takes 15% |
| 4. Safety & Conduct | 3 min | What to do if: no answer, package too large, safety concern, bad weather |
| 5. Quiz | 2 min | 5 multiple-choice questions; must score 4/5 to proceed |

#### Stage 5: First Drop (Target: within 48 hours of KYC)
- **Nudge system:** Push notification every 4 hours after KYC: "Drops available near you — complete your first one and earn £5 bonus!"
- **Buddy system:** Option to be paired with an experienced courier for first drop
- **First-drop bonus:** £5 credited immediately on completion
- **Support:** Real-time chat with ops team during first drop
- **Fallback:** If no organic drop available within 24 hrs, ops team creates a "training drop" (founder sends a package)

#### Stage 6: Staking Unlock (After 5 completed drops)
- **Requirement:** Deposit £50 refundable stake into platform escrow
- **Benefits unlocked:**
  - Priority matching (see drops 30 seconds before non-staked couriers)
  - Access to premium drops (merchant dispatches, higher-value packages)
  - Entry to tier system (Bronze → Silver → Gold)
  - Higher visibility on courier leaderboard
- **Stake payment:** Debit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay
- **Refund policy:** Full refund within 7 days of account deactivation, minus any outstanding claims
- **Psychological design:** By drop 5, courier has earned ~£25–40 and has validated the platform; staking feels like investing in a proven opportunity

## 6. Courier Economics

### 6.1 Earnings Model

| Variable | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Average delivery fee (set by courier) | £4.00–6.00 |
| Platform fee (deducted) | 15% = £0.60–0.90 |
| Net courier earnings per drop | £3.40–5.10 |
| Average drops per hour | 2.5–3.5 |
| **Hourly earnings range** | **£8.50–17.85** |
| **Expected average** | **£12–14/hr** |

### 6.2 Earnings Scenarios

| Courier Type | Hours/Week | Drops/Week | Weekly Earnings | Monthly Earnings |
