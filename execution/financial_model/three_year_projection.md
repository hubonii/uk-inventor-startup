# Three-Year Financial Projection — Local-Logistics Protocol

> Last updated: July 2026 | Basis: Expected Case Scenario

---

## Executive Summary

Over three years, Local-Logistics Protocol projects growth from **£36,773 in Year 1 revenue** to **£1,131,870 in Year 3**, achieving **monthly EBITDA breakeven in Q3 Year 2** and **full-year EBITDA positive in Year 3**. Gross margins improve from 67% to 71% as SaaS revenue share grows and infrastructure economies of scale kick in.

| Assumption | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
