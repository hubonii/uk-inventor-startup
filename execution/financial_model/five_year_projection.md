# Five-Year Strategic Projection — Local-Logistics Protocol

> Last updated: July 2026 | Long-Range Strategic Financial Plan

---

## Executive Summary

This five-year projection maps Local-Logistics Protocol from a **single-wedge London pilot** to a **pan-UK and international logistics infrastructure company**. By Year 5, the business targets **£12.8M annual revenue**, **320 employees**, operations in **25+ cities across 4 countries**, and **EBITDA-positive operations** from Year 4 onward. The model assumes Seed (£750k), Series A (£4M), and Series B (£12M) funding rounds.

---
