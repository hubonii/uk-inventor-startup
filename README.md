# P2P Urban Logistics Platform

Welcome to the central knowledge base and operating system for our green, sustainable peer-to-peer (P2P) urban logistics platform. This repository acts as the single source of truth for the entire startup, housing business plans, technical architecture, security protocols, market research, and legal documentation.

## 🚀 Concept Overview
An intent-based, crowdsourced delivery platform (similar to Uber but for packages). It allows anyone to deliver packages along their daily commute, focusing on eco-friendly, carbon-neutral transit utilizing existing human movement.

## 📁 Repository Structure

- `docs/` - Core startup documentation (Vision, Product, Architecture, Legal).
- `research/` - Market analysis, knowledge gaps, and research roadmaps.
- `reports/` - Generated diligence reports and project health checks.
- `agents/` - AI Personas (CEO, CTO, UK Lawyer, etc.) to evaluate and improve this startup.
- `assets/` - Images, diagrams, and static assets.
- `meeting-notes/` - Logs of strategic decisions and meeting transcripts.
- `decisions/` - Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).
- `archive/` - Deprecated ideas and historical documentation.
